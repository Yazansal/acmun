Our Story

ACMUN began as a vision to create a platform where young minds could engage with the most pressing issues of our time. Founded at Al Ahliyya College, our Model United Nations conference has grown into a prestigious event that attracts delegates from across the region.

Our mission is to foster diplomatic skills, critical thinking, and global awareness among students. Through realistic simulations of UN proceedings, participants develop leadership abilities, negotiation skills, and a deeper understanding of international relations.

Each year, ACMUN brings together brilliant students to debate, negotiate, and collaborate on solutions to global challenges. Our conference provides an authentic UN experience, complete with committee sessions, crisis scenarios, and diplomatic networking opportunities.

We believe in empowering the next generation of global leaders through hands-on diplomatic experience, fostering understanding across cultures, and building bridges between nations through meaningful dialogue.
